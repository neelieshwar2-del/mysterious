Vratam Peta Rental

What is Vratam Peta?

A "Vratam Peta" is a pre-arranged, sacred setup kit containing all the essential structural and metallic elements needed to perform a traditional Indian Pooja or Vratam (like Satyanarayana Vratam, Varalakshmi Vratam, or Ganesh Chaturthi) at home. Instead of buying expensive items that occupy storage space, you can rent them from us for 1-3 days.

Items Included in the Rental Kit:

  • 🪔 Traditional Wooden Peta: Handcrafted wooden pedestal base with ornate carvings.
  • 🪔 Background Backdrop Frame: Expandable metallic frame with matching red & yellow fabric drape.
  • 🪔 Auspicious Kalash: Premium brass/copper kalash pot with synthetic mango leaves insert.
  • 🪔 Metallic Diyas & Bell: Pair of 3-step brass peacock diyas and a resonant brass hand bell.
  • 🪔 Plate & Accessories: Complete brass aarti thali, panchapatra, pali set, and incense holder.
  • 🪔 Toran & Garlands: Reusable artificial marigold garlands and mango leaf toran for the main door entrance.

How the Rental Process Works:

  1. Book Online: Choose your required date in the booking form and submit via WhatsApp.
  2. Get it Delivered: We deliver the sanitized, polished kit to your doorstep the day before the pooja.
  3. Perform Your Ritual: Enjoy a beautiful, traditional setup without any hassle.
  4. We Pick It Up: We collect the kit the day after the pooja. Once checked, your ₹500 deposit is refunded instantly via GPay/PhonePe.
